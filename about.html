About Me

Hey, I'm Kaitlyn. As you may have guessed, this site has been created to represent... well, me! I will be giving a brief overview in the following pages to hopefully shine some of my life into yours.
Growing up and living in the midwest, my life has been pretty boring. As someone who grew up wanting to be a veterinarian, I would be quite surprised if someone told my younger self that the future would be how it currently is - working on getting a job as a front end web developer!

This journey so far has given me so much insight into how I learn the best, how to actually retain that education, and then put it to use (literally!) Some time after graduating high school while looking at online college degrees/certificates I could sign up for, I never thought Web Design would be something I would actively be doing. I always thought I wouldn't be creative enough for something like that. As of today, I am proving my past-self wrong; and I love what I found!
Although I did go through tutorial hell, I learned from it and utilized more of my resources to actually be able to learn what is needed.

I appreciate you for checking this out!

If there's ever one thing I can ask for, it is to be given a chance. The following pages will give a glimpse into how I got to where I am today. You are more than welcome to utilize the icons at the bottom of each page to get in contact with me. Whether it is comments, questions, or concerns - please let me know!
